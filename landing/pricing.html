Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the Community Edition commercially?

Yes, but you must comply with AGPL v3, which means open-sourcing your code if you provide RAGLint as a service. For commercial use without open-sourcing, get a Pro license.

What happens after the free trial?

After 14 days, you'll be charged $49/user/month. Cancel anytime, no questions asked.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. We'll prorate the charges.

Do you offer academic/non-profit discounts?

Yes! We offer 50% off Pro licenses for .edu emails and registered non-profits. Contact us at sales@raglint.com
